Executive Summary

Shrub Fund is a decentralized fund management protocol on Solana, combining professional trading with blockchain transparency. Users stake USDC to receive shares in a managed portfolio, with a 7-day unstaking period for liquidity. The protocol is live on Solana mainnet with a $15k test fund, demonstrating functional validation. Currently managed by a human Trader wallet, full automation is planned by Q1 2026.

1. Introduction

The Problem

  • High Barriers to Entry: Traditional funds require $100,000+ minimums
  • Opacity: Limited visibility into strategies and performance
  • Long Lock-ups: Capital tied up for 1-2 years
  • High Fees: 2% management + 20% performance fees
  • Centralized Risk: Single points of failure

The Solution

Shrub Fund offers:

  • No Minimums: Invest with any amount of USDC
  • Transparency: On-chain trades and NAV, trackable via DeFi tools
  • Liquidity: 7-day unstaking period
  • Low Costs: No management fees; performance fees planned
  • Decentralized Operations: Smart contract-based, managed by Trader wallet (full automation by Q1 2026)

3. Technical Innovation

Dual NAV System

Shrub Fund uses a one-directional NAV system:

  • Real NAV: Live market value for staking (premium pricing)
  • Optimized NAV: Smoothed increases (7-day average), instant decreases for unstaking (discount pricing), preventing gaming

Advanced Position Tracking

  • Spot cryptocurrency holdings
  • Jupiter Perpetuals (long/short)
  • Real-time P&L with fees
  • Cross-collateral derivatives planned

Scalable Registry System

  • 100,000 users per registry
  • Automatic, user-paid expansion
  • Efficient storage/lookup

6. Governance & Risk Management

Governance

Currently centralized via Gardener (program upgrades) and Trader (portfolio management). Decentralized governance planned for Phase 3 (Q1 2026) with governance token.

Risk Management

Smart Contract Risk:

  • One-directional NAV and 7-day unstaking prevent gaming
  • Hardcoded Gardener/Trader controls
  • Audits planned but not completed

Market Risk:

  • Diversified portfolio (tokens, perps)
  • Risk limits and monitoring
  • Stress testing planned

Operational Risk:

  • Real-time monitoring (pending cashouts, NAV updates)
  • Incident response protocols
  • Lambda failure risks (stale NAV)

Liquidity Risk:

  • 7-day unstaking buffer
  • Trader-funded PendingCashoutPool
  • Monitoring of cashout vs. trading balance
